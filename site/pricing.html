FAQ

Every question before you sign up

How does CostSage access my cloud?

Read-only IAM roles (AWS) or Azure RBAC Cost Management Reader permissions. We never request write access for analysis. Execution uses a separate, minimal-permission role you pre-approve and can revoke anytime.

What happens when I approve a recommendation?

The agent executes the change using a scoped execution role. Every action is logged in a full audit trail. Automatic rollback triggers monitor performance post-change — if anything degrades, the change is reversed automatically.

Is the trial really free with no credit card?

Yes — 14 full days, all features included, no credit card required. Connect your cloud account and see real recommendations from your actual infrastructure within minutes.

What if my cloud spend exceeds my plan?

We notify you when you approach your spend tier and you can upgrade at any time. We never cut off recommendations mid-month — your agent keeps working while you decide.

Do I have to auto-execute recommendations?

No — the default is always human-in-the-loop. Every recommendation requires your explicit approval. Enterprise plans can configure auto-approval rules for specific recommendation categories if you choose.

How long does setup actually take?

Most customers connect their first cloud account in under 60 seconds. The agent begins analysis immediately and surfaces its first set of recommendations within minutes — often before your first cup of coffee is done.
