3 Structural Themes Across All 9 Layers
🕳️
The Lead Capture Hole
The most expensive problem on the site: front-end forms capture intent, then do nothing with it. No backend, no CRM write, no nurture sequence. The checklist lead magnet links to "#" on three pages. Fix this first — everything else is optimising a broken funnel.
📣
Static Urgency Problem
All urgency signals are evergreen — the announcement bar, the urgency bar, the exit popup. Visitors who return see identical messaging and stop registering it as real. Urgency needs rotation, time-bounding, or dynamic proof counters ("47 teams joined this month") to stay credible.
🔗
Orphaned Pages Problem
Three high-value pages (nops-alternative, cloudzero-alternative, finops-agent-vs-dashboard) are not linked from the main nav, footer, or homepage body. They exist but receive no internal link equity and no organic discovery path from returning visitors. This is both an SEO and a funnel loss.
