Common Questions

Switching FAQs

Can I run CostSage alongside nOps during evaluation?

Yes — most teams do exactly this. Both tools use read-only API access during the initial phase, so there's no conflict. You'll have full visibility into both sets of recommendations during your free trial period, which makes the comparison straightforward.

Will CostSage ever make changes without my approval?

Only for actions you've explicitly pre-authorised in your guardrails configuration. By default, all actions require approval. You progressively grant the agent more autonomy as your confidence grows — starting with low-risk actions like tagging and scheduling, working up to rightsizing and commitment purchases.

How is CostSage's pricing different from nOps?

nOps charges a percentage of your committed-use savings, which scales with your cloud spend in ways that can feel disproportionate. CostSage is flat monthly pricing — you know what you pay, and every dollar the agent saves goes back to your business, not to us.

What about my existing nOps commitment management setup?

CostSage's agent has full RI and Savings Plan management capabilities, including purchasing and coverage monitoring. The transition is typically seamless — we ingest your current commitment inventory on day one and continue optimising from there.

Is CostSage safe for production workloads?

Yes. Production environments are protected by default — any action touching production requires explicit human approval. The agent will never autonomously resize, terminate, or modify production resources unless you've specifically configured it to do so.
